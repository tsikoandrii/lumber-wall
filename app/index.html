FAQs.

Do the panels come in larger sizes? The acoustic range panels only come in one size, 2400mm x 600mm. However, we have designed them to join both widthways and long ways seamlessly, allowing any length or width to be easily achieved.
Can the panels be used in a bathroom or kitchen? Yes, the panels will have no issues in these areas as long as they are not in direct contact with water.
How are the acoustic panels installed? To obtain a class A sound absorption, then we strongly recommend installing the acoustic wood panels with batons. Though, if you would like to have them solely for decorative purposes while still providing some sound absorption, we recommend screwing the panels directly into the wall through the acoustic felt backing using black or grey screws.
